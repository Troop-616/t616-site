Meetings Schedule

When We Gather

Scouting is built on consistency, youth leadership, and outdoor adventure. We hold our regular troop meetings the first three Wednesdays of each month at 7:00 PM.

School Year (September – May)

Meetings run from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Scouts attend in official Class A Uniforms.

Summer Program (June – August)

Meetings run from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Scouts attend in casual Class B Uniforms.

Note: To protect the safety of our youth, specific meeting locations are not published publicly on our website. Please contact us below to visit a meeting, or log in to your TroopWebHost account to view the calendar.
