Welcome to Troop 616

Over 55 Years of Scouting Excellence

Troop 616 was the first troop formed in Scripps Ranch. We have been active for over 55 years, helping scouts build character, learn life-long outdoor skills, and develop leadership capabilities.

Our program is scout-led, meaning the youth plan the meetings, coordinate outings, and run the troop. This hands-on experience prepares them for college, careers, and life.
