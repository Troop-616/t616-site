👕 Scouting Uniforms

Class A Uniform (Full Uniform)

Consists of the official khaki uniform shirt with appropriate insignia and patches, troop neckerchief and slide, scout socks, scout pants/shorts, scout belt, and merit badge sash. Required for weekly meetings during school year (September – May).

Uniform Inspection Sheet (PDF) →

Class B Uniform (Activity Uniform)

Consists of the red Troop 616 T-shirt with scout pants or shorts. Used for summer meetings (June – August), hikes, service projects, and traveling to/from camp outs.

Uniform Bank

Troop 616 maintains a uniform bank of gently used uniform pieces (shirts, shorts, socks) that members are welcome to take for free. If you would like to look for items, or donate clean items, see the Uniform Bank Coordinator.

🧑‍🏫 Adult Leader Training

Troop 616 requires adult volunteers and parent leaders to complete training to keep our youth safe and outings successful.
