📸 Upload & Ordering Guidelines

How to Upload Photos

Enter the Shutterfly site, click on the **Pictures** tab, and add your images to an existing album or create a new album. To sign in, use the shared troop email photos@t616.org and the troop password. Alternatively, you can use your personal Shutterfly account to sign in and contribute photos.

How to Order Prints

If you want to order physical prints or merchandise from an album, you must sign in with your personal Shutterfly account (or create a new free personal account). If you order under the shared troop account, billing information and transaction notifications will be sent to the troop coordinator instead of you.
